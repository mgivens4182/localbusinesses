Iowa City Apartments
Iowa City is one of the best places to live in the Midwest. It is also the home of the University of Iowa. Iowa City's main focus is on the culture and education. Every summer Iowa City hosts a jazz festival and an Iowa Arts Festival that features the state of Iowa's best visual art and music entertainment.Iowa City is often listed in "best places to live" studies because of the great public schools, safe and quite neighborhoods and a well educated population.

Iowa City and the surrounding areas have a total population of about 90,000 people when classes are in session at the University. There are over 33,500 students enrolled at the University of Iowa. The makeup of students is very diverse. The University of Iowa itself consists of eleven colleges, or fields of study. Many of the colleges are considered to be some of the very best programs in the midwest.

The life for a student at the University of Iowa is filled with things to do. The University provides many types of financial aid to both in state and out of state students. There are also resources for on-campus and off-campus housing. Many who graduate from the University of Iowa have excellent job prospects, both in state and in other parts of the U.S.
